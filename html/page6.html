As I walk back to my room I start to feel strange. Like I'm in a strangers body. But this is my body...

Seventeen...The pandemic started when I was that age. I turned eighteen that June. Graduated high school. Started college. All during lock down. All online. All within these four walls of my room. It was a lonely time but I got through it. Didn't I?

But it wasn't just lonely wasn't it? It was stagnate. Everyday I would wake up, go to my desk, and do nothing but scroll social media. Some days I wouldn't even make it out of bed. I thought that was over but isn't it what I just did today?

Sometimes it feels like I'm still seventeen. Still watching that shelter in place announcement. Still waiting for the day I finallygrow up.
